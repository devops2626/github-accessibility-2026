Chapter 1: Introduction

GitHub is the heart of open source. Yet millions of developers with disabilities face barriers daily. This book outlines the path to full accessibility by 2026.

Why Now?

  • Moral imperative
  • Untapped talent pool# Chapter 2: The Three Silent Barriers
